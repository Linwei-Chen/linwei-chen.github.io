陈林蔚

Linwei Chen

I received a B.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering and Automation from China University of Geosciences, Beijing, China, in 2019, and an M.S. degree in Software Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China, in 2021. I am currently pursuing an Eng.D. at MIIT Key Laboratory of Complex-field Intelligent Sensing, within the School of Information and Electronics at Beijing Institute of Technology. My research interests include image segmentation, object detection, and remote sensing.
